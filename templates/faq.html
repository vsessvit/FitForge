Frequently Asked Questions

Memberships

What membership options do you offer?

We offer three membership tiers: Basic, Premium, and Elite. Each tier provides different levels of access to our facilities, classes, and services. Visit our Membership Plans page for detailed information.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time from your account settings. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Absolutely! You can change your membership tier from your profile. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

Classes & Bookings

Do I need a membership to book classes?

Yes, an active membership is required to book any of our fitness classes. This ensures our members get priority access to our class schedules.

How do I book a class?

Browse the class schedule, select a time that works for you, and click "Book Now". You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I cancel a class booking?

Yes, you can cancel your bookings from the "My Bookings" page. We recommend canceling at least 2 hours before the class starts to free up the spot for other members.

What happens if a class is full?

If a class is at capacity, you can choose another time slot or check back later in case someone cancels. We frequently add new class times based on demand.

Shop & Products

Do I need a membership to shop?

No! Our shop is open to everyone. Browse fitness apparel, equipment, supplements, and accessories anytime.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. Memberships are non-refundable but can be canceled at any time.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure Stripe payment gateway.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use Stripe for payment processing, which meets the highest security standards (PCI DSS Level 1). We never store your card details on our servers.

When will I be charged for my membership?

You'll be charged immediately upon purchase, and then on the same date each billing cycle (monthly or annually, depending on your plan).

Facility

What are your opening hours?

FitForge is open 24/7 for Elite members, while Basic and Premium members have access from 5 AM to 11 PM daily.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! Personal training sessions can be booked separately. Elite members receive discounted rates on personal training packages.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available for all members and visitors.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? We're here to help!
